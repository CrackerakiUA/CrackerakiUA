Alongside this, I’ve worked part-time and on project-based engagements with NestJS, React, Vue, and PostgreSQL. I also have practical native experience with Java & Kotlin (Android), C#, and Swift, primarily while building Cordova and Capacitor plugins, bridging web technologies with native mobile platforms.

Outside direct commercial work, I actively code in Go and Rust, and work with Cosmos SDK, Ignite CLI, and Electron.js, building CLI tools, WASM modules, blockchain infrastructure, and desktop applications.

I’m a strong believer in open-source and MIT-licensed software. I’m the Founder & CEO of Web Art Work, creator of the wawjs framework, and co-founder of IT Kamianets. Over the years, I’ve mentored and guided 150+ students and junior developers, helping them enter and grow in the IT industry through real-world projects, architecture reviews, and career coaching.

I believe the industry is moving beyond strict roles like frontend, backend, or full-stack. In the near future, these roles will converge into a single Developer profession, capable of building across platforms and layers with the help of AI assistants — an approach I already apply when working with native mobile code, infrastructure, and blockchain systems.

My interests sit at the intersection of frontend architecture, scalable backends, cross-platform development, tooling, education, and community-driven tech ecosystems.

## 🧩 Author — wawjs framework

I use wawjs as both a production framework and a teaching tool, helping developers understand backend architecture, modular systems, and maintainable codebases.
